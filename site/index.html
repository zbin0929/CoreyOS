Core Capabilities

Three AI Robots, One Operations Hub

Each robot monitors a critical aspect of your Amazon business 24/7, pushes alerts to your IM, and executes actions with your confirmation.

☃

Ad Guardian

7x24 monitoring of your advertising campaigns. Identifies wasteful keywords, suggests negatives, and executes with one-click confirmation.

  • Wasteful keyword detection (high clicks, zero conversions)
  • ACOS threshold alerts pushed to IM
  • One-click negative keyword execution
  • Semantic relevance analysis via AI
☷

Inventory Sentinel

Monitors FBA inventory levels and sales velocity. Predicts stockout risks and recommends replenishment timing.

  • Stockout warning (<14 days yellow, <7 days red)
  • Replenishment priority ranking
  • Aging inventory alerts (90+ days)
  • Daily inventory health briefing via IM
★

Profit Analyst

Calculates per-SKU profitability by combining Amazon settlement reports with your cost data from ERP.

  • Per-SKU profit breakdown (FBA, ads, referral, COGS)
  • Profit margin threshold alerts
  • Automated weekly/monthly reports
  • ERP integration via secure database connection
Getting Started

Up and Running in Three Steps

01

Install & Authorize

Download CoreyOS for macOS or Windows. Authorize your Amazon Seller Central account via secure OAuth. Your SP-API credentials are stored in your OS keychain — never in plaintext.

02

Connect Your Data

Link your ERP or inventory system via a read-only database connection. CoreyOS only reads — it never modifies your business systems without your explicit confirmation.

03

Activate Robots

Enable the AI robots you need. They start monitoring immediately and push actionable insights to your preferred IM channel (WeChat, DingTalk, Feishu, or Slack).

Data Protection

Your Data, Your Machine, Your Control

Unlike cloud-based SaaS tools, CoreyOS processes everything locally. No data ever leaves your network.

✓

Local-First Architecture

All data processing and storage happens on your machine. No cloud uploads, no third-party data sharing.

✓

OS Keychain for Credentials

API keys and database passwords are stored in macOS Keychain / Windows Credential Manager — never in config files.

✓

Read-Only by Default

CoreyOS reads your Amazon and ERP data for analysis. Any write operation (negative keywords, price changes) requires your explicit confirmation.

✓

Official API Only

We only use Amazon's official Selling Partner API (SP-API). No scraping, no unofficial data sources.

Data Flow Architecture

Amazon SP-API (official, read-only) ↓ CoreyOS (your machine) ↓ AI analysis (local LLM) ↓ Actionable insights Your IM (WeChat / DingTalk / Feishu)

Your ERP (read-only DB connection) ↓ CoreyOS (your machine) ↓ Combined with SP-API data ↓ Profitability analysis

No data flows outward. No cloud. No third parties.
